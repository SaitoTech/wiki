Wordblocks

Wordblocks is a crossword puzzle game, spell out words on the board to earn points. The tiles must form words that, in crossword fashion, read left to right in rows or downward in columns and are included in a standard dictionary or lexicon. 

The game is played by two to four players on a square game board imprinted with a 15Ã—15 grid of cells (individually known as "squares"), each of which accommodates a single letter tile. In official club and tournament games, play is between two players or, occasionally, between two teams, each of which collaborates on a single rack.

The board is marked with "premium" squares, which multiply the number of points awarded: eight dark red "triple-word" squares, 12 orange "double-word" squares, of which one, 8 dark blue "triple-letter" squares, and 20 green "double-letter" squares.

 

Currently, you can play in the Saito arcade on English (two dictionaries), SOWPODS or TWL0 or Spanish.
