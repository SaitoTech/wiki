Tutorial 3 - Arcade and Red Square Wallet Management

Saito wallets or accounts are based and stored on the browser's cache they are currently running, for that so, even in the same device (PC, MAC, mobile), every different browser that opens Saito will have its own wallet, same happens when incognito tabs. 

Many users that are not used to blockchain technology, struggle to understand how this works and why they are the guardians and responsible for their own wallets and keys. Saito's wallets do work as the other crypto wallets (cold, hot, or even hardware), the user will have two keys, the Public key, which is one that anyone can see, this key is displayed on chat or in the Red Square posts if there is not a username register yet, and can be used to receive coins in the future. The other is the Private key, which should be kept safe, if possible not online, since with this key the wallet can be restored on any browser or device, in case of loss, if the user does not have a backup of the wallet or the private key, no one will be able to help them to restore and gain access back to this wallet/account. 

Backup and Restore

Go to Saito Arcade or Saito RedSquare, and open the “hamburger menu" or the 3 lines at the top right of your screen.

Here you you will see a menu, in this case the RedSquare, with part of the controls for your wallet, a QR and your public key, an option to create a game invites, if you click on account, we can move to the how to backup or restore your wallet.

First to reveal and save your private key, click on the eye icon (1) then copy the characters (2) that will show in a text file, or even better on paper. If you click on “backup wallet” (3), this will download a file to your computer that contains the info to restore your wallet (4), the 5th option there is to import your private key (2) and it's the 2nd way to restore your wallet. 

Register a username 

Saito usernames are unique, if they have been taken by another account/wallet, you won't be able to use them, the same applies in the case of a username that was registered but you lost access to it (lost private key and/or backup file), since no one will be able to restore it, that username will be lost with the wallet. 

To register a username, follow the instructions for the backup and restore, Saito Arcade/RS > Hamburger menu > Account > (6) Register a Username.
