Token Supply

The Saito Network has 7bb SAITO tokens and a zero-inflation policy. Because Saito is a non-inflationary token, the only way to get tokens is to purchase them from other holders or run network servers and earn them in exchange for routing transaction fees into the network. This makes Saito tokenomics fundamentally different than proof-of-work and proof-of-stake mechanisms that require token inflation to pay for network services and use issuance and lock-up games to debase holders.

While most tokens in circulation exist on that main network, approximately 30 percent of Saito tokens started circulating in the form of ERC20 / BEP20 tokens. To assist users migrating these chains onto the network, the project supports a bridge that holders can use to migrate these tokens to mainnet at any time. Please note that this is the only officially-supported way of migrating tokens to mainnet. If someone proactively contacts you and offers to assist or asks you to use another method it is a scam.

If you hold ERC20 or BSC Saito you can earn interest from liquidity provision (a form of staking) on off-chain exchanges. On-chain token balances are eligible for ATR rewards through Saito Consensus, which redirects 25% of on-chain fee payments into a treasury that is distributed to token holders through the security mechanism.
