Tokenomics

The Saito Network has 7bb SAITO tokens and a zero-inflation policy. Because Saito is a non-inflationary token, the only way to get tokens is to purchase them from other holders. In addition to being useful on the network, on-chain balances are a automatically eligible for payouts from Saito's ATR mechanism, which distributes 25% of on-chain fees to holders of unspent UTXO at regular intervals.

The easiest way to purchase Saito is through our on-chain store. It is also possible to purchase SAITO tokens at a discount by buying our ERC20 or BSC-wrapped tokens and migrating your tokens to mainnet at any time. Please note that this bridge is the only officially-supported way of migrating off-chain SAITO tokens to mainnet. If someone proactively contacts you and offers to assist it is a scam.
