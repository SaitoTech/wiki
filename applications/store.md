# Store
  
[The Saito Store](https://saito.io/store/) is a decentralized e-commerce application that runs atop the Saito Network. It allows sellers to list items for sale, and buyers to buy them. All sales are processed directly on-chain as atomic swaps. There is no point where sellers lose control of their listings.

This makes the Saito Store different from private websites and marketplaces, where users have to transfer ownership. Instead of taking possession of items, the Saito Store merely indexes the listings that users make. Selling an item does not involve "uploading" it to the control of the server but making a transaction that allows it to be transferred under certain conditions.

## Decentralized Moderation

Given that the Saito Store is a decentralized application that simply indexes listings made by users as transactions on a public blockchain, the Saito team cannot vouch for any listings posted on user stores. We encourage users to show appropriate caution when purchasing items from third-parties.

As an index to blockchain-mediated transactions, we do not take any stance on the reversibility of transactions. We do not condone the sale of illegal or morally odious content and will review and remove listings from our index. If you run into content that is clearly inappropriate and/or in violation of shared human decency, please flag it for review.
