## Themes

Where are the themes? We have kept the theming engine but moved themes to NFTs. 
Having a single theme means we can focus on building a high quality and consistent user experience. Having themes as NFTs showcases the very different kind of things that are possible with Saito NFTs and introduces people to the store.

We'll be introducing example themes at saito.io/store and providing examples so that anyone can see how simple it is to create, and sell, themes.
