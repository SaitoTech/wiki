Saito Arcade

The Saito Arcade is an open-source game engine that runs as a fully-distributed peer-to-peer (p2p) blockchain application. All the games you can play run directly in the browser using p2p communications to send and receive game moves. Players interact only with each other - there is no central trusted server for deals or storing game data.

This is one of the first open-source software developed by the Saito team. It has been recognized by the Web3 Foundation as a standard for open cryptographic gaming.  Also, the arcade works as a showcase for the games/app/chat/forum / AppStore / etc. These are all applications that run in the browser and send-and-receive on-chain data.

Twilight Struggle

Cold war-based card-driven strategy game for two players! Twilight Struggle is an award-winning Strategy game set in the Cold War. Two players face each other as one of the world’s superpowers (the US and the USSR) and try to spread their influence across the world, with a heavy focus on the use of event cards that can make or break your strategy.

Poker

Texas Hold'em Poker for the Saito Arcade. With five cards on the table and two in your hand, can you bet and bluff your way to victory?

The Saito Community hosts a Chess League anyone can join at any time.

Chess

A Saito Blockchain implementation of Chess, one of the oldest and most popular board games, in which two players use several different types of pieces in order to force their opponent’s most important piece, the King, into a position in which it cannot avoid capture, called Checkmate.

Wuziqi

Wuziqi also called Five in a Row/Gomoku and Gobang, traditionally played with Go pieces on a 15x15 board. Wuziqi is a strategy boardgame played on a Go board using black and white stones. Players take turns placing stones of their color on the board, whoever manages to make an uninterrupted row of five stones wins.

Red Imperium

Red Imperium is an epic Sci-Fi strategy game. Up to 6 players compete to conquer the galaxy and seize control of the collapsed Imperium’s former seat of power, New Byzantium, and thus crown themselves as the legitimate rulers of the Known Universe. The Saito Community hosts a weekly Introductory League to teach new players everything about Red Imperium from how to play to how to handle its political intrigues in chill, no pressure setting. Find out more [here], we look forward to seeing you around.

Quake 3

Quake3 is a multiplayer first-person shooter originally released by ID Software in 1999. This version runs directly in your browser and connects with Saito to use other Saito applications and bring modules right into the game.

NOTE: Quake 3 is owned by ID Software. This module is made available under an open source license. Your browser will use data-files distributed freely online but please note that the publisher requires the purchase of the game to play. Saito recommends GOG.com for purchase.

Wordblocks

A crossword puzzle game in which players use letter tiles to spell out words and accumulate points. Race to claim the bonus squares on the board before your opponent and set up tricky word setups to block your opponent, all is fair game in Wordblocks.

Settlers of Saitoa

Explore the island of Saitoa, collect resources, and build your way to dominance. You’ve landed on an uncivilized island paradise, it’s now up to you to settle this island and build your civilization faster than your rivals by exploiting its bountiful resources. Build roads, cities, and trade to assert your dominance and become the ruler of the island of Saitoa.

Shogun

Strategy deck-building game: Shogun is a deck-building strategy game, the goal is to increase your wealth to accumulate so that you can assert your dominance over opposing warlords and seize control of the shogunate.

Epidemic

Cooperative multiplayer board game in which players try to save mankind by picking one of several unique classes and then race to find the cure to deadly viruses before turn into global epidemics and destroy civilization.

Spider

Two-deck solitaire card game that traps you in a web of addiction. The main purpose of the game is to remove all cards from the table, assembling them in the tableau before removing them.

Blackjack

Classic casino game with home rules. Try to get closest to 21 without going over and beat the dealer to win your bet, but look out! You may be dealer next hand.
