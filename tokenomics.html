Token Supply

The Saito Network has 7bb SAITO tokens and a zero-inflation policy. Because Saito is a non-inflationary token, the only way to get tokens is to purchase them from other holders or earn them in exchange for routing transaction fees into the network. On-chain token balances are eligible for ATR rewards, which redirects 25% of on-chain fees into a treasury that is distributed to holders of unspent UTXO at regular intervals.

If you hold ERC20 or BSC-wrapped Saito tokens you can earn interest from liquidity provision (a form of staking) on off-chain exchanges, or migrate your tokens to mainnet at any time. Please note that this bridge is the only officially-supported way of migrating off-chain SAITO tokens to mainnet. If someone proactively contacts you and offers to assist or asks you to use another method it is a scam.
