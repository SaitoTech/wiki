Saito Mania

Blast shitcoins, pick up superpowers, destroy rocks to collect Saito, and learn about the Saito project while playing ;).  It's a space-themed multidirectional shooter arcade video game. The player controls a single Saito spaceship in an asteroid and shit-coins field. The object of the game is to shoot and destroy the asteroids and shitcoins, while not colliding with either, or being hit by them. The game becomes harder as the number of objects on the screen increases.
