# Automated Token Migration

tl:dr; - go to saito.io/migration and follow the prompts.
Note: The **automated migration** prompt is loaded after connecting to the migration node and confirming balance. This can take a few seconds.

---

## Automated migration:


|---|---|
| Navigate to saito.io/migration<br>Wait for **_automated migration_** to appear.<br>Click It. | ![image.png](/image.png) |
| Send the ERC 20 saito to the address shown.<br>*This is a unique address for your local browser wallet.*<div>This address is your Saito Wallet's Ethereum network address. If you had not enabled ETH previoulsy it is generated on the fly.</div>|![screenshot_from_2025-09-23_23-32-57.png](/screenshot_from_2025-09-23_23-32-57.png)|
| The transfer will take 16 confirmations on the Ethereum network<br> so may take some time.<br>Enjoy feline entertainments.<div>At this point your ERC20 SAITO is held in your Saito Wallet. It is imporant to have backed up your wallet, before beginning this process.</div>|![screenshot_from_2025-09-23_23-46-50.png](/screenshot_from_2025-09-23_23-46-50.png)|
|Once the ERC20 Saito is received you will be prompted to convert it to native SAITO.<div>Clicking *Convert* sends the ERC20 SAITO to the conversion bot (bridge) which will confirm receipt and return the Native SAITO tokens.</div>|![screenshot_from_2025-09-23_23-49-02.png](/screenshot_from_2025-09-23_23-49-02.png)|
| The overlay will close and you will receive a site notification when the SAITO arrives.|![screenshot_from_2025-09-23_23-49-17.png](/screenshot_from_2025-09-23_23-49-17.png)|
| Once the Saito arrives you can view your balance in your wallet.<br>Enjoy!|![screenshot_from_2025-09-23_23-50-33.png](/screenshot_from_2025-09-23_23-50-33.png)|

> If you have any trouble during the process, contact the team on [Red Square](saito.io/redsquare) or migration@saito.io
